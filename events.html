Annual Gala Auction

Our annual auction is both a fundraising and entertaining event. School families and community members participate in a variety of ways: by donating, acquiring items to auction, planning and managing the event, and by being present the night of the auction. If you would like to make a donation to this event, please contact Meredith McSwain, Director of Advancement, at mmcswain@morgantondayschool.com.We look forward to celebrating our 41st Anniversary and recent purchase of the Old Armory with current families, staff, alumni (students and families), and community members. If you are interested in becoming a sponsor, donating an item/service, or attending, please contact Meredith McSwain, Director of Advancement, at mmcswain@morgantondayschool.com. Please also contact Mrs. McSwain with address changes and/or to add your address to our invitation list. We hope you will join us on Saturday, February 22, 2020, for our 41st Annual Gala Auction!

Touch a truck

Our 1st annual Touch a Truck family event was a great success! Thank you to all of our truck operators, sponsors, vendors, first responders and public safety officers! The date has been set for next year’s event – save the date: Please join us Saturday, October 24th from 10 am to 2 pm at the old armory building on McDowell Street in downtown Morganton. Rain date is Nov. 7th. Parking will be across College Street at the CoMMA. 10am to 11am will be Sensory Hour, with no loud noises or flashing lights. Children and children at heart can explore tons of large trucks and vehicles, meet the heroes who operate them, as well as enjoy hourly activities, games, face painting, food trucks, and a vendor fair. Come bring your friends and join the fun! Information will be posted here in the fall about activities and ticket purchase, so be sure to check back.

Feast

We celebrated our annual Thanksgiving Day Feast in the Old Armory with family and friends. Children in grades K-8 prepared food with host families at various locations and returned to campus to enjoy a large meal together.

Grandfriends day

Come and join us for Grandfreiends day on May 7th, 2020. Grandfriends day is an annual celebration of our grandfriends! Grandfriends day is all about activities, food, and our students' annual art show! Students can bring grandparents, grandfriends, or other family.
