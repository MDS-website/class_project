Admissions



Morganton Day School admits families and students who embrace the philosophical foundations of inquiry-based education, parental involvement, and community service. The school seeks to build a community of students and families with a broad range of backgrounds and perspectives in order to enhance ethnic richness and diversity.



We are pleased that you are interested in pursuing admission to Morganton Day School for your child. Admission to MDS is both accessible and competitive as enrollment is open throughout the year if a vacancy exists and the admissions criteria are met.



Just as parents have expectations of MDS, the school, likewise, has expectations of parents and students. Steps for enrollment are established to assure clarity of communication, and the securing of adequate information for wise decision-making.
